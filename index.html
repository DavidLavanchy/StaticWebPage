Code Brew's Mission

Code Brew is a 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Organization designed to close the access to education and experience gap for underrepresented groups in tech. For every bag of coffee you purchase, 10% of proceeds will be donated to help fund local initiatives that help expose these marginalized communities to technology and programming. Please help us in our mission to impact lives through coffee and coding!
